Numerical Intergration Methods

Using Numerical Intergration Methods to Calculate Constants and Uncertainty Values in Quantum Mechanics on Jpyter Notebook

This was the project I carried out as a part of my university coursework. It involves me using three numerical intergration methods (Trapezium rule, Simpson's Rule and Monte-Carlo intergration) to calculate the expectation values of position and their uncertainties. Then finding the unknown constants in the wavefunctions. The aim was to find the best integration method for the all 3 parts of the wave function.
